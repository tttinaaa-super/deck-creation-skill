## Detailed Layout Guidance

### Title Slide
- Full brand logo or name
- Campaign tagline / Big Idea
- Subtitle: campaign period or `Draft for discussion`
- Subtle atmospheric background

### Executive Summary (Overview grid)
- 4-6 cards in a 2x3 or 3x2 grid
- Each card has a bold number + short sentence
- At most 10 words per card

### Statement / Quote Slides (Challenge, Insight)
- One strong sentence in large type (48-64px)
- Decorative quote mark or geometric accent
- Minimal supporting text below
- Full-bleed background

### Content Slides (Business Context, Brand Role, Audience)
- Left: heading + body text
- Right: decorative shape, gradient, or chart area
- Max 80 words of body text

### Grid / Table Slides (Objective, Channel Architecture, Measurement)
- 3-4 column layout
- Each column has an icon + heading + 1-2 lines
- Clean, structured spacing

### Timeline Slides (Rollout Plan)
- Horizontal timeline with 3 phase markers
- Each phase: label, duration, 2-3 key actions
- Connecting line or arrow

### Closing Slide
- Brand mark
- Campaign tagline repeated
- CTA line (contact / next step)
- Simple, elegant

## Design Tokens for Marketing Decks

When generating slides, use these defaults unless overridden by style selection:

- **Font scale**: Title 72px, Section heading 48px, Body 24px, Caption 18px
- **Animation**: Slight staggered reveal (0.1s delays), slide transitions via fade
- **Max words per non-reading slide**: 60 words / 4 bullet points
- **Max words per reading slide**: 120 words / 8 bullet points
